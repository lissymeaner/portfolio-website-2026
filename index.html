About Me

In case it wasn't obvious, I'm Affie, or Fie. Welcome. I'm a freelance web developer hailing from the U.K. and a first-year CS student at Northampton. I specialise in making multi-page, fully secure websites using platforms such as Shopify and WordPress. With full client support and enhanced security.

Professionally, I would love to get in touch with every single viewer that visits my website - not that many have questions, I find. However, if you have any, feel free to contact me by clicking here, the hamburger menu on top, or on the nav bar, if you're on desktop.
